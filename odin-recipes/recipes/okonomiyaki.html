Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 2 cups Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp pureed ginger/ Ginger Powder optional
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded green cabbage
  • 2 spring onions(I had not use here but mostly use in authentic recipe)
  • 2 Chilies thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt as per taste

Instructions

  1. Except oil mix all ingredients together
  2. Apply little oil on flat pan.Spread batter in desire shape.
  3. Cook it on medium flame.
